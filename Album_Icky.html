ALBUM

ICKY

2023.05.23

KARD debuts their electrifying album 'Icky' featuring the captivating title track 'Electric Shock' set to release on November 30th!

  • 'Icky', their inaugural studio album, encapsulates the essence of KARD's dynamic musicality, showcasing a fusion of their distinct styles and raw energy. Title track 'Electric Shock' is a pulsating anthem, blending addictive beats and powerful vocals, produced by the acclaimed team of high-profile composers, including Big Matthew and BM, the group's own talented members.

  • The album unfolds with 8 tracks, each a testament to KARD's versatility and creativity. From the soulful 'Burning Desire' to the infectious rhythm of 'Dazed Dreamer', 'Icky' promises a diverse sonic journey that fans, affectionately known as Hidden KARD, eagerly anticipate.

  • 'Electric Shock' music video embodies KARD's signature charisma, captivating viewers with sleek visuals and a narrative that amplifies the song's intensity. With stunning choreography and magnetic performances, KARD showcases their multifaceted artistry, cementing their position as trendsetters in the global music scene.

  • KARD's 'Icky' album represents a bold step forward, showcasing their evolution as artists while staying true to the group's distinctive style. With their groundbreaking sound and unparalleled stage presence, KARD continues to solidify their status as trailblazers in the K-pop industry.

KARD's 'Icky' marks a pivotal moment in their career, highlighting their individuality while reinforcing their collective strength as a group. Fans can expect an exhilarating album that pushes boundaries and leaves an indelible mark in the realm of K-pop.
