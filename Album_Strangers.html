ALBUM

STRANGERS

2023.05.23

KARD's BM Unveils Debut Solo Album 'Stranger' and Lead Single 'Lost in the Echo' on October 10th!

  • BM reveals his inaugural solo album 'Stranger', a collection epitomizing the multifaceted essence of 'BM,' merging his distinct musical flair and enigmatic charm. The title track 'Lost in the Echo' encapsulates a compelling fusion of rhythmic beats and emotive tones

  • With 'Stranger,' BM delves into unexplored sonic landscapes, offering a glimpse into his inner world and musical ingenuity. As KARD's first member to embark on a solo venture, BM sets the bar high, promising an extraordinary journey both within the group and as a standalone artist.

BM's naturally charismatic and bold musical persona resonates passionately through 'Stranger,' underscoring his commitment to delivering an album that resonates with his audience's hearts while solidifying his stature as a multifaceted performer in the K-pop landscape.
