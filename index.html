Welcome to the official website of KARD, the trailblazing co-ed K-pop group under DSP Media. Formed in 2017, KARD, comprised of J.Seph, BM, Somin, and Jiwoo, brings a unique fusion of tropical house, hip-hop, and EDM to the global music scene.

With hits like "Oh NaNa" and "Hola Hola," KARD has captivated audiences worldwide, known not only for their dynamic sound but also for their high-energy performances. Breaking stereotypes and embracing innovation, KARD has become a driving force in the international K-pop community.

Explore our site for the latest updates, exclusive content, and a glimpse into the diverse world of KARD as we continue to push musical boundaries and captivate audiences around the globe.
